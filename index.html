About Us

Singapore Beverage

The concept of a quality product made in Nepal is almost obsolete when it comes to non-basic products. We all have, over the years, so rigidly convinced ourselves that if anything is ‘Made in Nepal’, it’s of inferior quality, or inferior technology, or inferior taste, and so on. Nepal is so much dependent on imports that we no longer trust our native products. We need cars made in America or Japan, machines made in Germany, but we have the least trust in our local product.
